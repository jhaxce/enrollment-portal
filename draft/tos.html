Welcome to MSU-GSC Enrollment System!

Our online enrollment system provides a convenient and efficient way for students to enroll in their desired courses, pay tuition fees, and view their grades and academic records.

With our system, students can easily browse through our course catalog, check course schedules and availability, and enroll in their preferred courses without the hassle of manual enrollment procedures.

Our system also provides faculty members with an easy way to manage their classes, view student records, and communicate with their students.

Thank you for choosing MSU-GSC Enrollment System. We are committed to providing you with the best enrollment experience possible.
